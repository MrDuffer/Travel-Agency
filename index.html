How travel agency works

Travel agencies, which can be private retailers or public services, help people plan and book travel arrangements. They work with travel suppliers, such as airlines, hotels, and cruise lines, to offer a variety of travel packages for different destinations. Travel agencies can also act as general sales agents for airlines that don't have offices in a specific area.
